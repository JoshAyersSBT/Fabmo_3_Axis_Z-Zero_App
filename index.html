3-Axis Z-Zero Plate Tool

How to use your 3 Axis Z-Zero Plate

C10-Messure Outside Corner

Use this button to measure the outside corner of the material. Place the Z-Zero plate in the appropriate position and press this button to begin the measuring process.

C11-Messure-Inside-Corner

Use this button to measure the inside corner of your material. Make sure the Z-Zero plate is properly positioned before pressing the button.

C12-Calculate-Contact-Diameter

This button calculates the contact diameter based on the points you have measured. Ensure all necessary corners are measured before using this button.
